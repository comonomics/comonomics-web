About Comonomics

Commerce Fest

COMONOMICS is a celebration of knowledge, innovation, and the dynamic world of Commerce and Economics. Here we provide a series of engaging competitions, through which the participants will have the chance to demonstrate their expertise and exchange ideas.

Location

Mount Carmel school
Dwarka,Sector-22

Time

9am - 1pm

COMONOMICS 2023

The inaugural edition of COMONOMICS 2023 attracted a vibrant community of 300 participants from 30 schools, making a remarkable gathering of young minds eager to delve into realms of Commerce and Economics.

It was a testament of the festival's growing influence and enthusiasm of students across various educational institutions.

Event Details

We have a line-up of five events that will challenge and engage participants.

On-site Online

Launch-pad

Launch-pad is a start-up based event, where participants are required to create an idea for start-up-launch-pad

    Accounting Artistry

    The theme of the competition revolves around various accounting principles and assumptions.

      Mind Over Matter

      The quiz covers a wide range of topics, such as general knowledge, current affairs, history, geography, science, literature, music, sports, commerce and economics etc.

        Financial Wizards

        The quiz will focus on themes like Budgeting, Banking, Investments, Insurance, Taxation, Cyber security and Current business trends featuring a mix of question formats like MCQ, Direct text questions, arrange the order and Buzzer round.

          Economics Premier League

          The Economics Premier League(EPL) is a competitive event where participants tackle case study on economics problems.
